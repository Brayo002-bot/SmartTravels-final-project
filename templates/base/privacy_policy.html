🔐 Privacy Policy

Effective Date: {{ date|default:"2026" }}

SmartTravels is committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our transport booking system.

1. Information We Collect

  • Name (for ticket identification)
  • Phone number (for STK payments and ticket delivery)
  • Travel details (route, seat, time)
  • Location data (only for drivers during live trip tracking)

2. Why We Collect Your Data

We collect your data strictly for the following purposes:

  • To process bookings and issue valid tickets
  • To enable M-Pesa STK push payments
  • To track journeys for safety and real-time updates
  • To manage passenger manifests for drivers and transport operators
  • To award loyalty points for frequent travelers
  • To improve service reliability and customer experience

3. No Account Required

Passengers are not required to create an account or log in. We only store minimal booking data needed to complete your journey and send your ticket.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell your data. Information is only shared with transport operators (bus, train, flight) to ensure smooth travel operations.

5. Data Security

Your data is protected using secure servers, encrypted transactions, and restricted access systems.

6. Driver GPS Tracking

Drivers are tracked in real-time during active journeys to improve passenger safety and trip monitoring. Passenger tracking is only active during the trip and can be stopped when a passenger alights.

7. Contact

If you have questions about this policy, contact SmartTravels support.
