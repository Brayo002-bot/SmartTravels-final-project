01. Acceptance of Terms

By initializing access or interacting with the SmartTravels environment, you validate a binding agreement to these systemic conditions. If you choose not to concede to these operational boundaries, please do not use the platform.

02. Booking System

SmartTravels operates a dynamic digital routing structure enabling passengers to allocate and lock transport seating capacities. This sequence requires unique account validation. All travel vectors are finalized only upon successful downstream financial clearance.

03. Payments Architecture

All financial transactions are directly handled via synchronized M-Pesa STK Push telemetry. Secured seat inventory allocations and active e-tickets are explicitly labeled invalid until positive payment callbacks match downstream system records.

04. Passenger Responsibilities

  • Maintain and provide an accurate, active telephone index for encrypted SMS ticket data streams.
  • Arrive securely ahead of the declared system departure window to guarantee validation.
  • Consistently follow all safety protocols declared by individual fleet operators.

05. Driver & GPS Tracking

To maintain fleet safety and operational visibility, logistics units are tracked continuously during active trips. Client geo-location processing is restricted exclusively to managing passenger boarding coordinates and alighting confirmations.

06. Loyalty Incentives

SmartTravels automatically balances network loyalty balances for active profiles following successful completions of itineraries. These point structures are non-transferable and can only be used for platform-approved fare adjustments or promotional benefits.

07. Cancellation Framework

08. System Integrity & Usage
