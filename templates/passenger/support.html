Frequently Asked Questions

How do I redeem loyalty points?

Visit the Loyalty page and choose an available travel reward. Points can be redeemed for bus, train or flight discounts.

How do I cancel my booking?

Go to My Bookings and select the ticket you wish to cancel. Refund policies vary depending on the transport company.

What payment methods are supported?

SmartTravels supports M-Pesa, debit cards, credit cards and mobile banking.

Can I send parcels through SmartTravels?

Yes. Select the parcel delivery option and choose your preferred transport company.
