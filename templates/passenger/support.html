Frequently Asked Questions

How do I redeem loyalty points?

Visit your personal Loyalty page and select a travel reward ticket perk tier. Points apply instant system price write-offs at the checkout point.

How do I cancel my active booking?

Open My Bookings panel, open up your details receipt element and opt for cancellation procedures. Terms apply by unique line operators.

What payment methods are supported?

SmartTravels connects with integrated M-Pesa channels, direct banking card rails, credit portals, and digital points balances perfectly.

Can I send parcels through SmartTravels?

Yes. Use the explicit package delivery dashboard module to create structural drops, select a cargo provider vehicle, and generate tracking stamps.
